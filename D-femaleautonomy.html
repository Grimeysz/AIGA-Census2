63% of designers who feel they don't have freedom are women.

Although women make up 70% of the current design industry, they continue to feel overpowered.

The design industry has more female representation than ever; but the lack of attention on their perspectives remain stagnant and disproportionate. With growing emphasis on social issues regarding women themselves, how can designers help fix these issues without listening to those whose thoughts matter most?
