Women in design only make 80% of what male designers do.

Though the wage gap is age-old discrimination, it persists even in female-dominant spaces too.

Although there is a growing presence of women in the design field, the industry fails to materialize this importance in their pay. Inequities in the workplace are detrimental to the positive impact that representation should be having.
